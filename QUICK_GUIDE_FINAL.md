## 📋 How to Use Resume Builder

1. **Add Sections:**
   - Look at left sidebar
   - Find "02 / MANUAL COMPONENT MODULES"
   - Click any button (📝, 💼, 🚀, 🛠️, 🎓, 🌐, 📜, 🏆)
   - Fill in your content in "03 / ACTIVE FIELDS"
   - See live preview on right

2. **Download Resume:**
   - Look for "⬇️ EXPORT & DOWNLOAD" in left sidebar
   - Click "📄 Download as PDF" or "🖨️ Print"
   - File downloads/prints instantly!

---

## 📄 How to Use Cover Letter Builder

1. **Edit Content:**
   - Edit name, role in "01 / YOUR INFO"
   - Edit company, contact in "02 / RECIPIENT DETAILS"
   - Edit letter paragraphs in "03 / LETTER PARAGRAPHS"
   - See live preview on right

2. **Download Letter:**
   - Look for "⬇️ EXPORT & DOWNLOAD" in left sidebar
   - Click "📄 Download as PDF" or "🖨️ Print"
   - File downloads/prints instantly!
