💡 DOWNLOAD FEATURE DETAILS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

How Download Works:

Option 1: Direct Download (Recommended)
  • Click: 📄 Download as PDF button
  • Wait: 1-2 seconds
  • Result: PDF downloads to Downloads folder
  • Filename: [YourName]_Resume.pdf or [YourName]_Letter.pdf
  • Quality: Professional high-quality PDF

Option 2: Browser Print (Fallback)
  • Click: 🖨️ Print button
  • Dialog Opens: Browser print dialog
  • Select: "Save as PDF"
  • Choose: Location and filename
  • Result: PDF saved where you choose

Both methods produce professional PDFs! ✅
